# Zenist

English | [繁體中文](README_zh-TW.md) | [简体中文](README_zh-CN.md)

---

**Zenist** is a minimalist task management application designed for focus. 

The core philosophy of Zenist is built upon three principles:
* **Simple**: A pure and distraction-free interface.
* **No Push Notifications**: Respecting your attention without unwanted interruptions.
* **Bring Your Own Cloud**: Your data is synchronized entirely through your personal cloud drive (Dropbox), ensuring absolute privacy and ownership.

### Features
* **Minimalist UI**: Designed with a clean palette to keep you focused on what matters.
* **Background Execution**: Minimizes to the system tray for seamless operation.
* **Single Instance**: Optimized to run lightly on your system without duplicate processes.
* **Offline First**: Works entirely offline with a local database, syncing quietly when connected.
